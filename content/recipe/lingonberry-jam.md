## Ingredients

* 2 dl of water
* 0.5 kg of sugar
* 1 kg lingonberries

## Method

1. Boil berries and water for 15-20 minutes.
2. Add sugar and stir until sugar has melted. Boil another 5 min.
3. Foam and sprinkle the jam into cans.
